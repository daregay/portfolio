Predicting Cardiovascular Disease

Tech used: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Scikit-Learn: Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Logistic Regression, K-nearest neighbor, Stacked classifier.
Description: Found the best classification predictive model that detects the presence of cardiovascular disease. Compared four optimized models, stacking the models yielded a recall score of 87%, which was higher than the individual models. Given the sensitivity of the study, it was imperative that the model does a good job of capturing every person with cardiovascular disease, so recall was chosen as the best metric because it penalizes false negatives.

    Includes:
  • Data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, and hyperparameter tuning methodologies.
  • Anlysis of AUC-ROC curve, confusion matrix, feature importance, partial dependency, decision boundries, and PCA.
